Welcome to Oxford Kiddies Academy!

At Oxford Kiddies Academy, we believe every child deserves a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where they can grow into confident, curious, and happy learners.

Our programs are designed to support each child’s unique developmental journey through fun, play-based learning and structured activities. We focus on building strong foundations in creativity, problem-solving, language, and social skills.

We are more than just a childcare center—we are a family. Our passionate teachers work closely with parents to ensure that every child feels supported, valued, and encouraged to reach their full potential.

Whether it’s exploring new ideas, making friends, or discovering talents, Oxford Kiddies Academy is the perfect place for your child to thrive. Together, we create memories, friendships, and a love for lifelong learning.
